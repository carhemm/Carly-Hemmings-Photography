About Me

That's Me (Carly Hemmings)

My name is Carly Hemmings and I am a photographer based in Bel Air, Maryland. I have been taking photos for over 9 years and have a passion for capturing moments that tell a story. I specialize in sports, landscape and pet photography, but I also enjoy experimenting with different styles and techniques. My goal is to create images that evoke emotion and inspire others to see the world in a new way.
